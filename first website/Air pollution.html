Air Pollution
Pollution and its types When some foreign particles particles enters the air and deteriorate its quality is known as Air pollution .These particles can be produced naturally or man made.Dust storms,Bacteria and gases of volcanic eruptions are examples of natural's sources of pollutants.Gases released from the automobiles and the chimneys are example of man made pollutants.

Major air pollutants: Carbon monoxide(CO),Ozone(O3),Nitrogen dioxide(NO2),
Sulphur dioxide(SO2) and lead.
